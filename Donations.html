The Paw Pack is an initiative led by the University of East Anglia, to transform Norwich into a safe city for all animals. The Paw Pack Pet Adoption Center is located at the University of East Anglia, in the heart of Norfolk. Paw Pack, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, operates the Norfolk's largest sanctuary for homeless animals; provides adoption, spay/neuter, and educational programs.
